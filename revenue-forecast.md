## Part 2: Revenue Model

### Revenue Streams

#### Stream 1 — Affiliate Commissions (Passive)
- Triggered when user views optimized schedule and clicks a travel link
- Commission: 3–6% on hotel/flight bookings
- Avg booking value: $400 (US) / ₩320,000 (~$240) Korea
- CTR from results page: 8–15%
- Booking conversion of clicks: 2–4%
- **Effective revenue per MAU per month: $0.15 – $0.45**
- Base case used in model: **$0.25/MAU/month**

#### Stream 2 — Premium Subscription
- Price: $6/month or $49/year (US) · ₩7,900/month (Korea, ~$5.90)
- Features: calendar sync, alerts, multi-destination, PDF export
- Conversion rate: 3% of MAU (conservative), 6% (optimistic)
- Blended price: **$5.80/subscriber/month** (mix of monthly + annual)
- Launch: Month 10

#### Stream 3 — Direct Sponsorships
- CPM basis: $10–$20 for high-intent travel audience
- Estimated impressions: 4.5/user/month (3 page views × 1.5 sessions)
- Flat-fee deals start at ~$500–$2,000/month for regional brands
- Launch: Month 10–12 (requires proven traffic to pitch)
- Base case: **$8 CPM effective**

#### Stream 4 — (Year 2+) B2B / HR Licensing
- Sell annual license to HR teams / employee benefits platforms
- Price: $500–$5,000/year per company
- Not modeled in Year 1 — included in Year 2–3 upside
