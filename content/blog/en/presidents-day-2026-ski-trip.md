## The Mid-Winter Reset You Need

By February, the holiday glow has faded and spring feels far away. Presidents' Day — always the third Monday in February — is your built-in midwinter escape hatch.

In 2026, Presidents' Day falls on **Monday, February 16**. That's a guaranteed 3-day weekend for anyone whose employer observes it (most do — it's a federal holiday).

![Calendar showing the Presidents Day 2026 PTO window: 2 PTO days on February 12-13 combined with the weekend and holiday for 5 days off](/blog/presidents-day-calendar.svg)

## Extending to 5 Days

Take Thursday and Friday off before the long weekend:

| Date | Day | Status |
|------|-----|--------|
| Thu Feb 12 | Thursday | **PTO day 1** |
| Fri Feb 13 | Friday | **PTO day 2** |
| Sat Feb 14 | Saturday | Weekend (Valentine's Day) |
| Sun Feb 15 | Sunday | Weekend |
| Mon Feb 16 | Monday | Presidents' Day |

**2 PTO days → 5 days off. Efficiency: 2.5x.**

Valentine's Day falls on Saturday this year — which means you can combine a romantic getaway with the long weekend without using extra PTO.

## Why This Is the Best Ski Weekend

Presidents' Day weekend is peak ski season, but there's a nuance:

**The weekend itself (Sat–Mon) is packed.** Lift lines are long, lodging is at premium pricing, and highways to mountain towns are bumper-to-bumper.

**Thursday and Friday before it are not.** By arriving Thursday, you get two full days of skiing with weekday crowds and weekday lift ticket prices (often 20–30% less). By the time the weekend warriors arrive Saturday, you've already logged your best runs.

**The secret play:** Ski Thursday–Saturday, rest Sunday, enjoy the empty mountain Monday morning when everyone else is driving home.

## Best Ski Destinations for a 5-Day Trip

![Destination cards for Presidents Day ski trips: Park City, Breckenridge, Jackson Hole, and Palm Springs](/blog/presidents-day-destinations.svg)

**Western US:**
- **Park City, Utah** — Consistent February snow. Two resorts (Park City + Deer Valley) in walking distance. SLC airport is 35 minutes from the slopes.
- **Breckenridge, Colorado** — February averages 10+ inches of snowfall. The town has enough restaurants and shops to fill your non-ski hours.
- **Jackson Hole, Wyoming** — For advanced skiers. February is peak powder season. Less crowded than Colorado resorts.

**Eastern US:**
- **Stowe, Vermont** — The best skiing on the East Coast. February is reliably cold with good coverage. Fly into Burlington (BTV), 40-minute drive.
- **Killington, Vermont** — Largest ski area in the East. More affordable than Stowe, more terrain variety.

**Non-ski alternatives:**
- **Palm Springs, CA** — 75°F in February. Desert hiking, mid-century architecture, natural hot springs. The anti-ski trip.
- **Puerto Rico** — No passport needed. 85°F, beach season. Flights from the East Coast run $200–$350 round trip in February.
- **Scottsdale, AZ** — Golf season peak, hiking weather perfection, spa culture. A completely different kind of reset.

## Booking Strategy

Presidents' Day weekend is one of the most expensive ski weekends of the year. Here's how to beat the pricing:

**Lodging:** Book by early January. Slopeside hotels sell out 6–8 weeks before Presidents' Day. Airbnbs in nearby towns (not slopeside) are 40–50% cheaper.

**Lift tickets:** Buy in advance online. Most resorts offer 10–20% off for tickets purchased 7+ days ahead. Multi-day passes (4–5 day) have better per-day rates than singles.

**Flights:** Fly Thursday morning, return Tuesday (skip the Monday exodus). This window is significantly cheaper than the Fri→Sun pattern.

**Rental cars:** Mountain town rentals spike for Presidents' Day weekend. Reserve early or consider shuttle services from the airport.

## For Non-Skiers

Not a ski person? February is still a great time for a 5-day trip:

- **Museum + food trip** — NYC, Chicago, or DC in February means no crowds at major museums and easy restaurant reservations. Bundle with a show or concert.
- **Southern road trip** — Savannah → Charleston → Asheville. Mild winter weather (50–60°F), beautiful architecture, fewer tourists.
- **Mexico City** — Dry season, 70°F, one of the world's great food cities. 5 days is ideal for neighborhoods, museums, day trips to Teotihuacán.

## The Full-Year View

Presidents' Day is a great early-year trip because it uses minimal PTO (just 2 days) and breaks up the February monotony. After this, your next major window is Easter (April) or Memorial Day (May).
