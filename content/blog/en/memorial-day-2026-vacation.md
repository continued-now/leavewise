## The Summer Kickoff Window

Memorial Day 2026 falls on Monday, May 25. Most people get a 3-day weekend and call it good. With a little planning, you can turn it into a proper 9-day vacation.

## The Play

Take Tuesday through Friday off the week before Memorial Day:

| Date | Day | Status |
|------|-----|--------|
| Sat May 16 | Saturday | Weekend |
| Sun May 17 | Sunday | Weekend |
| Mon May 18 | Monday | Regular workday |
| Tue May 19 | Tuesday | **PTO day 1** |
| Wed May 20 | Wednesday | **PTO day 2** |
| Thu May 21 | Thursday | **PTO day 3** |
| Fri May 22 | Friday | **PTO day 4** |

**4 PTO days → 9 days off (May 16–25). Efficiency: 2.3x.**

Alternatively, take the week *after* (May 26–29) for the same 9-day result but with post-holiday travel pricing, which tends to be 10–15% cheaper.

## The Budget Option

Just need a quick reset? Take Friday May 22 off.

**1 PTO day → 4 days off (Fri–Mon). Efficiency: 4.0x.**

That single Friday before Memorial Day is one of the highest-value PTO days of the year.

## Where to Go in Late May

Late May sits in a sweet spot: warm enough for beaches, not yet peak summer pricing.

**Beaches without the June markup:**
- **Puerto Rico** — No passport needed for US citizens. San Juan has direct flights from most East Coast cities. Late May weather is ideal (low 80s, pre-hurricane season).
- **Outer Banks, NC** — Peak season doesn't start until June. Late May means uncrowded beaches at shoulder-season rates.
- **San Diego** — "May gray" keeps some tourists away, but temperatures are still in the 70s. Hotel prices are 20–30% below July rates.

**International shoulder season:**
- **Greece** — Islands like Santorini and Crete are warm but not yet overrun. Direct flights from East Coast cities are available, and hotel rates are 40% below July.
- **Portugal** — Lisbon and the Algarve coast in late May: 75°F, dry, uncrowded. One of Europe's best-value destinations year-round.
- **Costa Rica** — The "green season" (rainy) starts in May, but mornings are sunny and prices drop 30%. Nine days is plenty for Pacific coast + cloud forests.

## Booking Strategy

Memorial Day travel is predictable. Here's the pricing curve:

- **Now (April):** Best time to book. Domestic flights are at baseline.
- **Early May:** Prices start climbing. Popular routes (NYC→Miami, LAX→Hawaii) jump 15–20%.
- **Week before:** Last-minute domestic flights are expensive. International may still have deals.

Hotels in beach destinations book up fast for Memorial Day weekend specifically — but the preceding week (May 16–22) has great availability since most people only take the long weekend.
