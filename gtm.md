# PTO Optimizer — Go-To-Market Plan
### 1 Person · US + Korea · 12 Months

---

## Mission

**EN:** Help every working professional squeeze the most life out of their time off — without gaming the system, just using what they already have.

**KO:** 이미 주어진 공휴일과 연차를 최대한 활용해서, 더 많이 쉬고 더 잘 살 수 있도록 돕는다.

| Quarter | MAU Target | Revenue Target | Primary Source |
|---------|-----------|----------------|----------------|
| Q1 | 500–2,000 | $0–$2K | None (build phase) |

## Growth Philosophy

> Passive first. Affiliate links and SEO do the heavy lifting. You write content once, it earns forever. Sponsorships and premium come later once traffic is proven.

**Priority order:**
1. Affiliate links (automated, zero maintenance)
2. Organic SEO — English (Google) + Korean (Naver)
3. Community seeding (Reddit, Blind)
4. Direct sponsorships
5. Premium subscription

### Months 5–6: SEO & Content

**Goal:** Build organic traffic that compounds. Write it once, rank forever.

#### Step 9 — English Blog Posts (publish on your own domain)

Publish one post every 2 weeks. Target these exact titles (high search volume, low competition):

1. **"Best Days to Take PTO in 2026 (Maximize Your Long Weekends)"**
   - Target keywords: best days to take PTO, maximize vacation days 2026
   - Content: list every US long weekend opportunity in 2026 with the math

2. **"How to Turn 10 PTO Days Into 18 Days Off (2026 Calendar)"**
   - Target keywords: how to maximize PTO, turn PTO into longer vacation
   - Content: walkthrough of the best stacking opportunities

3. **"Memorial Day, July 4th, Labor Day: The Ultimate 2026 Long Weekend Guide"**
   - Target keywords: long weekend 2026, Memorial Day vacation, July 4th time off
   - Content: specific booking windows and travel tip CTAs (affiliate links here)

4. **"The Best Time to Use Your Vacation Days in 2026 (By Month)"**
   - Target keywords: when to take vacation 2026, vacation planning 2026
   - Content: month-by-month breakdown with affiliate hotel/flight links embedded

**SEO setup:**
- Submit sitemap to Google Search Console (search.google.com/search-console)
- Add meta descriptions to every post
- Internal link all blog posts back to the tool

#### Step 10 — Korean Naver Blog Posts

Post on your Naver Blog. These titles target high-volume Korean search queries:

1. **"2026년 연차 황금연휴 총정리 — 언제 쓰면 제일 길게 쉬나요?"**
   - 키워드: 연차 언제 쓸까, 황금연휴 2026, 연차 효율적으로 쓰기

2. **"연차 10일로 18일 쉬는 방법 (2026 달력 기준)"**
   - 키워드: 연차 최대한 활용, 공휴일 연차 끼우기

3. **"설날 추석 연차 계획 — 2026년 가장 긴 연휴 만들기"**
   - 키워드: 설날 연차, 추석 연차 계획, 명절 연휴 늘리기

4. **"2026 월별 연차 추천 날짜 — 직장인 필수 가이드"**

### Months 7–9: Affiliate Activation

**Goal:** Turn traffic into passive revenue.

#### Step 11 — Embed Affiliate Links Contextually

Place affiliate links at the highest-intent moment: **after the user sees their optimized schedule.**

Example flow:
> "You could take off [dates] and get 9 days off for only 4 vacation days. ✈️ Want to find flights for this window?"
> → [Search flights on Skyscanner] ← affiliate link
> → [Find hotels on Booking.com] ← affiliate link

**Korean version:**
> "[날짜]에 연차 4일만 쓰면 9일 연속으로 쉴 수 있어요! ✈️ 이 기간 여행 계획 세워볼까요?"
> → [항공권 검색 (스카이스캐너)] ← affiliate link
> → [숙소 찾기 (야놀자 or 인터파크)] ← affiliate link

#### Step 12 — Track & Optimize

Monthly check (30 min max):
- [ ] Which affiliate links got the most clicks?
- [ ] What's the conversion rate? (clicks → bookings)
- [ ] Which blog posts are driving the most tool usage?
- [ ] Top 3 traffic sources this month?

Use: Google Analytics (free) + each affiliate dashboard
